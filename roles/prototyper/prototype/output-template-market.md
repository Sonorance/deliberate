# Prototype

## What it tests

- **Hypothesis:** _The audience, positioning, offer, package, price, or channel assumption being tested._
- **Touchpoint:** _Where the intended customer encounters the artifact._
- **Claim and proof:** _What the artifact promises and the grounded proof it presents._
- **Action:** _The observable customer response the artifact invites._

## Test path

- **Entry:** _How the intended audience reaches or receives the touchpoint._
- **Response:** _The reachable action, objection, alternate response, or failure state._
- **Observation:** _What a reviewer or real test would observe without mistaking mocked behavior for evidence._

## Grounding and fidelity

- **Reused conventions:** _The real brand, product, offer, and copy conventions followed._
- **Mocked elements:** _The data, interactions, prices, or other details clearly treated as mocked._

## Decision signal

- **Scale if:** _The evidence that supports the market choice._
- **Revise or stop if:** _The evidence that invalidates or changes it._
