# Launch

## Audience

_Who this is for, the job or buying situation, and why this audience matters._

## Commitment

_The positioning, offer, channel, or commercial move being made. State the promise, boundary, and explicit non-goals._

## Proof

- **What already supports it:** _Grounded evidence or capability._
- **What still needs testing:** _The most important uncertainty and the simplest credible test._

## Sequence

- **Now:** _The first concrete action._
- **Next:** _What follows if the first action supports the direction._
- **Not yet:** _What remains deferred or out of scope._

## Measure

_The one most useful progress or downside signal, if grounded._

## Revisit

_The evidence, date, or market change that should trigger a fresh decision._
