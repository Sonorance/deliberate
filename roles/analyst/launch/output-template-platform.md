# Launch

## Decision

_The build, buy, adopt, integrate, migrate, partner, standardize, or dependency choice being made and why._

## Boundaries

- **Included:** _Systems, users, workflows, or partners covered._
- **Excluded:** _What remains outside the decision._
- **Required compatibility:** _The constraint or promise that must hold._

## Proof

- **What already supports it:** _Grounded technical, operational, partner, or ecosystem evidence._
- **What still needs testing:** _The most important uncertainty and how to test it._

## Adoption

- **Now:** _The first adoption, integration, or migration action._
- **Next:** _What follows if compatibility and value hold._
- **Fallback:** _How to pause, reverse, or contain the decision._

## Measure

_The one most useful adoption, reliability, interoperability, or downside signal, if grounded._

## Revisit

_The evidence, dependency change, or ecosystem condition that should trigger a fresh decision._
