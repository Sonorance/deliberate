# Launch

## Direction

_The strategic choice and why it can improve the product's position._

## Commitments

- **Do:** _The few actions or investments required by the choice._
- **Stop or decline:** _What will no longer receive attention, funding, or priority._
- **Protect:** _A capability, customer promise, or option that must remain intact._

## Sequence

1. **Now:** _The first commitment._
2. **Next:** _What follows after the first evidence or milestone._
3. **Later or conditional:** _What remains contingent._

## Proof

- **Evidence to seek:** _What would show the strategic logic is working._
- **Warning sign:** _What would weaken the choice._

## Revisit

_The milestone, evidence, or external change that should trigger a fresh strategic choice._
