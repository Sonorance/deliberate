# Frame

## Decision

**Decision to make:** _The unresolved market or commercial choice, stated without prescribing the answer._

**Trigger:** _The concrete signal or change that makes the decision timely._

## Audience and current motion

| Audience / buyer | Job or desired outcome | Current alternative or perception | Evidence |

## Current market context

- **Positioning / category:** _How the product is understood today, when grounded._
- **Offer / packaging / pricing:** _The relevant current commercial choice, or “Not established by the sources.”_
- **Distribution / sales motion:** _The relevant current route to the audience and evidence of reach._

## Constraints and stakes

- **Constraints:** _Product truth, strategy, trust, business-model, or channel constraints._
- **Cost of no decision:** _What maintaining the current approach risks or leaves unresolved._
- **Evidence gaps:** _The material unknowns that limit the decision._
