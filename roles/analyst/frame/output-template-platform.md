# Frame

## Decision

**Decision to make:** _The unresolved build, buy, adopt, integrate, migrate, partner, standard, or dependency choice._

**Trigger:** _The concrete product, technical, ecosystem, policy, pricing, security, or availability change._

## Current system and relationship

- **Affected users and systems:** _Who or what bears the outcome._
- **Current approach:** _The present architecture, provider, protocol, partner, or workaround._
- **Dependency exposure:** _Where control, lock-in, availability, policy, or partner incentives matter._

## Evidence

- _Grounded user, operational, technical, partner, or market evidence._
- _A second material signal, or an explicit evidence gap._
