# Frame

## Decision

**Decision to make:** _The consequential strategic or portfolio choice._

**Trigger:** _The grounded change, tension, or opportunity that makes it timely._

## Diagnosis

_What is happening, why it matters to the project objective, and the underlying cause rather than the visible symptom._

## Evidence

- _A grounded quantitative or qualitative signal, with its source._
- _A second material signal, or an explicit evidence gap._

## Current direction

- **Objective:** _The project objective this decision must advance._
- **Current commitments:** _The choices and resource commitments already in force._
- **Constraints:** _Non-goals, trust boundaries, resources, dependencies, or timing._
- **Cost of the status quo:** _What happens if the current direction continues._
